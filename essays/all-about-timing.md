Throughout the extent of this final project, I've come to a mildly intriguing conclusion. I'm frankly really bad at effort estimation. I tend to severely overestimate the complexity of an issue and estimate an uncomfortable amount of time to complete it. Most of the time, I've proven myself wrong once I've actually taken on a task. "Oh this wasn't as bad as I thought" has been a common thought resurfacing in my head every time I got through a problem.
